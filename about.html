Want to know more about us?

Hi there! Welcome to our Ciabatta Business. We take our business in bread very seriously. Let's start at the beginning.

In 1987, my grandmother came over to the U.S. from Italy. She was the best bread maker in all of Trieste. She started out by selling out of her tiny apartment. Through word of mouth, she built up her business and made enough money to come over to the States and build on her dream.

Unfortunately, she became very ill in 1997 and handed the business to her nephew, Chad, and it's been running under his guidance since then.

Everything is the same as Nonna made it. The recipe has not changed. We take a lot of pride in our fresh ingredients and don't want to shy away from tradition.

After all, we are family. La famiglia e tutto!
